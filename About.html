Hi! My Name is Santiago Perez Clavier and im a Graphic designer.
Being a versatile Graphic Designer and Visual Communicator has led me to learn about many specialties, including Web Design and 3D Animation. Self taught and very passionate about games, i have come to love game development and have taken part, along with a personal team, in some interesting demo proyects using Unity 3D. Also, over the last 3 years i’ve been working as a freelance web designer for IT Companies, such as 4Geeks and ACCEDE, here on Venezuela. By doing this, it was possible for me to pay for my studies on Prodiseño, a prestigious Visual Communicators institute here on Caracas. My dream is to make games, by working on an important Game Development Company, or by creating my own company, as long as i partake in making great games.
2D Software: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, After Effects, Dreamweaver.
3D Software: Maya, Zbrush, 3Ds Max, Marmoset, Topogun.
